Where We Work

Juba, South Sudan

Konnect Africa's office in Juba, South Sudan, is strategically located to serve clients in the region. Our team here is dedicated to making a positive impact in South Sudan and supporting our clients' goals and initiatives.

Nairobi, Kenya

In Nairobi, Kenya, Konnect Africa operates from a dynamic hub that allows us to engage with clients throughout East Africa. Our team in Nairobi specializes in delivering tailored solutions and services to promote development and growth.

Khartoum, Sudan

Our presence in Khartoum, Sudan, allows us to collaborate closely with organizations and communities in Sudan. We work on various projects to enhance governance, peacebuilding, and development in the region.

Dar El Salam, Tanzania

Our office in Dar El Salam, Tanzania, is strategically positioned to support initiatives in the East African region. We collaborate with local communities and organizations to foster development, good governance, and sustainable growth.

Ethiopia, Addis Ababa

Located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, our team is committed to advancing social, economic, and political progress in Ethiopia and the surrounding areas. We work on projects that promote positive change and inclusivity.

Uganda, Kampala

Konnect Africa's presence in Kampala, Uganda, allows us to actively engage in projects aimed at improving the lives of Ugandans. We collaborate with local partners to address key development challenges and opportunities.
